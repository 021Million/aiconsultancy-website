AI Training

AI training and workshops for businesses and teams.

Practical workshops for businesses, schools, clinics, and organisations who want to understand how AI can be used clearly, responsibly, and effectively. Tailored to your team, your industry, and your starting point.

Why it matters

Teams that understand AI adopt it better.

AI tools are only useful when people know how to use them. Most teams are not using AI because they do not know where to start, what tools are worth the time, or how to use them safely.

These sessions change that. By the end, your team has a clear picture of what AI is, what it can and cannot do, and how to start using it in ways that are practical and relevant to your work.

No assumed technical knowledge. No jargon. Just clear, practical guidance that helps people leave the session ready to act.

  • No technical background required
  • Tailored to your industry
  • Practical focus — tools people will actually use
  • Includes time for questions and real examples
  • Available in person or online
  • Half-day, full-day, or multi-session formats

What we cover

Workshop topics.

Sessions are built around what your team needs. Choose from the topics below, or request a fully custom session.

AI 101

A clear, jargon-free introduction to AI — what it is, how it works, and what it means for your business right now.

  • What AI actually is (and what it isn't)
  • How modern AI tools work
  • What AI is genuinely good at
  • Where AI has limits and risks
  • Why now is the right time to engage with it

Practical AI tools for daily work

A hands-on session covering the AI tools most useful for business productivity — including how to use them effectively.

  • AI writing and communication tools
  • AI for research and summarisation
  • Prompting — how to get better results
  • AI tools for your specific role or industry
  • When to use AI and when not to

Responsible AI and privacy

A focused session on how to use AI safely, what data risks exist, and how to set responsible guidelines for your team.

  • What data AI tools use and store
  • Privacy considerations by tool
  • How to set sensible AI use policies
  • Human oversight — what it means and why it matters
  • Real risks versus overstated ones

AI for leadership teams

A strategic session for decision-makers. How AI is changing business, where the opportunities are, and how to approach implementation.

  • The business case for AI adoption
  • How to identify high-value AI opportunities
  • Managing AI adoption across your team
  • Questions to ask before investing in AI tools
  • What good AI implementation looks like

Finding AI opportunities in your business

A practical working session where we map your workflows together and identify where AI could save time, reduce admin, and create value.

  • Workflow mapping exercise
  • Identifying repetitive and manual tasks
  • Prioritising AI opportunities by value
  • Practical next steps for each opportunity
  • What to build versus what to buy

Custom workshops

A session designed entirely around your team, your industry, and your specific questions. Reach out and we'll build something that fits.

  • Industry-specific AI use cases
  • Tool-specific training for what you already use
  • Team adoption strategy sessions
  • Multi-session learning programmes
  • Any combination of the above topics
Who it is for

Training designed for real teams.

These sessions work for any size business or organisation. The content is adapted to who is in the room.

Healthcare teams

Clinics, practitioners, admin and support staff. Focused on practical tools, privacy considerations, and what AI means for patient interactions and admin workflows.

Schools and education

Teachers, leaders, and support staff. Covers AI literacy, practical tools for educators, responsible AI in schools, and how to approach AI as an opportunity rather than a threat.

Business owners and teams

Small and medium businesses across any industry. Practical focus on time-saving tools, workflow identification, and how to get started without the overwhelm.

Leadership and executives

Strategic sessions for decision-makers. Focused on the business landscape, adoption strategy, and how to lead AI integration across an organisation.

Admin and operations teams

Teams spending significant time on process and admin. Focused on automation opportunities, practical tools, and how to reduce manual work without adding complexity.

Any team, any starting point

Whether your team has never used an AI tool or they are already experimenting — sessions are designed around where people actually are, not where they should be.
