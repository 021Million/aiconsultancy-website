The pattern

You've seen this before.

Every decade, one technology separates the businesses that grow from the ones that fall behind. It has happened four times in your lifetime. It is happening again right now.

1990
The Internet

Get online or get left behind. Businesses with a website thrived. Those without it became invisible.

2000
Google Search

Get found or become irrelevant. Digital presence stopped being optional. SEO became the battlefield.

2010
Social Media

Build an audience or lose your market. Businesses that showed up won trust. Those that ignored it fell behind.

2020
Artificial Intelligence

Automate or fall behind. The businesses acting now are building an advantage that will compound every year.

The businesses that acted early in each era won. This time is no different.
