Where AI helps

AI solutions for trades and service businesses.

Quote request assistants

An AI tool that captures quote requests from your website , collecting the job details, location, timeline, photos, and contact information , and hands it to you in a ready-to-respond format. No more back-and-forth gathering basic information.

More complete quote requests. Faster turnaround. Less time on discovery calls before you can price the job.

Job intake and scheduling

Automated intake workflows that collect job information, confirm bookings, send job reminders to customers, and notify your team of upcoming work. Reduce the back-and-forth of scheduling manually.

Fewer no-shows. Cleaner scheduling. Less time on the phone coordinating bookings.

Customer follow-up automation

Automated follow-up sequences for after a job is completed , checking in on the customer, asking for a review, offering related services, or prompting a rebooking. Set up once, runs for every job automatically.

More Google reviews. Higher repeat booking rate. Customer relationships maintained without manual effort.

Invoice and payment follow-up

Automated invoice reminders at set intervals , so chasing outstanding payments does not fall on your team's to-do list. Polite, professional, and consistent without anyone having to remember to send them.

Faster payment collection. Less awkward manual chasing. Reduced outstanding invoices.

Customer enquiry chatbot

A chatbot on your website that answers common questions about your services, coverage areas, pricing ranges, booking process, and availability , 24 hours a day. Captures contact details from serious enquiries even when you are on a job.

Leads captured outside business hours. Fewer missed enquiries. Customers get answers immediately instead of waiting.

Admin and communication support

AI drafting tools that help you write job updates, quote cover letters, service reports, and customer communications faster. Templates trained on your business so they sound like you , not like a generic email.

Less time writing communications from scratch. More professional, consistent customer communications.
