Where AI helps

AI solutions for admin-heavy teams.

Inbox management and triage

AI that reads, categorises, and drafts responses to incoming emails , handling routine enquiries, routing complex issues to the right person, and drafting responses to common message types for your review and approval.

Inbox processed faster. Time spent on email reduced significantly. Nothing important gets missed or delayed.

Document drafting and generation

AI-assisted drafting for proposals, reports, letters, meeting agendas, and internal documents. You provide the key information, the AI produces a well-structured draft , you review and finalise. Faster than starting from scratch every time.

Documents produced faster. More consistent quality and formatting. Less time staring at a blank page.

Meeting notes and action tracking

AI that transcribes, summarises, and extracts action items from meetings. Key decisions, next steps, and who is responsible , all pulled out automatically and sent to the right people without anyone having to type up notes after the fact.

Time saved on note-taking and distribution. Clearer action tracking. Fewer things falling through the cracks after meetings.

SOP and knowledge assistants

A private AI trained on your standard operating procedures, policies, guides, and documentation. Team members get instant answers to process questions , without interrupting colleagues or digging through folders to find the right document.

Faster access to information. More consistent application of procedures. Onboarding new staff takes less time.

Data entry and reporting automation

Automate the manual transfer of data between systems, the extraction of information from documents and forms, and the generation of recurring reports. Work that takes hours done in minutes , and done consistently, without errors.

Hours returned to the team per week. Data handled consistently. Reporting produced without manual effort.

Repetitive communication workflows

Automate the predictable communications , reminders, acknowledgements, status updates, follow-ups, and confirmations , that your team sends manually dozens of times a week. Once set up, these run automatically in the background.

Consistent communications without manual effort. Team freed up from repetitive sending tasks. Nothing falls through the cracks.
