Where AI helps

AI solutions for education providers.

AI 101 workshops for staff

In-person or online workshops for teachers, leaders, support staff, and administrators. Covers what AI is, what it can and cannot do, how to use it responsibly in an education context, and what tools are worth exploring. No assumed technical background.

Staff confident and informed. Consistent understanding across the organisation. Practical skills your team can use immediately.

Teacher productivity tools

Help teachers use AI to draft lesson plans, create resources, give feedback on student work faster, generate assessment rubrics, and communicate with parents more efficiently. More time for teaching, less time on preparation and admin.

Hours returned to teachers per week. Better-quality resources created faster. Reduced preparation burden.

Admin and enrolment automation

Automate enrolment enquiries, application follow-ups, fee reminder sequences, and routine parent or student communications. Handle the repetitive, predictable admin without it eating into staff time.

Faster enquiry response times. Fewer manual follow-up steps. Admin staff freed up for higher-value work.

Resource and document assistants

A private AI assistant trained on your curriculum documents, policies, and guides. Staff find what they need instantly , without hunting through shared drives or asking a colleague every time.

Faster access to the right information. More consistent application of policies and procedures.

Internal knowledge systems

Turn your existing documentation , policies, handbooks, procedures, and guides , into a searchable, conversational knowledge base. New staff onboard faster. Existing staff spend less time asking colleagues for information they should already have access to.

Faster onboarding. Reduced dependence on institutional memory. More consistent practice across the organisation.

Communication support

AI-assisted drafting for newsletters, parent communications, announcements, and reports. Faster to produce, more consistent in tone, and easier to adapt for different audiences.

Consistent communications. Less time drafting from scratch. Communications that get out on time.

AI training for schools

Running AI workshops for your school or team.

We run in-person AI training sessions designed specifically for education environments. Sessions are adapted for different audiences , teachers, leadership teams, support staff, and board members , and focus on what is practically relevant to your context.

Sessions address the questions that come up in every school conversation: what do we do about AI in assessments, how do we talk to students about it, what tools are appropriate for staff to use, and what does responsible AI adoption look like in an education setting.
