Where AI helps

AI solutions for healthcare clinics.

AI reception support

An AI assistant on your website that handles common patient enquiries, answers FAQs about your services and fees, provides directions and hours, and directs people to the right booking process , 24/7, without your front desk needing to respond.

Fewer phone interruptions. Faster responses for patients outside business hours.

Patient follow-up workflows

Automated follow-up sequences for appointment reminders, post-treatment check-ins, re-booking prompts, and care plan completion reminders. Set up once and they run consistently for every patient , no manual tracking needed.

Higher re-booking rates. Less time chasing patients manually. Better continuity of care.

Inbox and admin automation

Triage and respond to common email and enquiry types automatically. Appointment requests, referral information requests, insurance queries, and routine communications handled without your team needing to read and respond to every message.

Less time in the inbox. Faster response times. Admin team freed up for higher-value tasks.

Internal clinic knowledge tools

A private AI assistant trained on your clinic's protocols, pricing, team processes, and FAQs. New staff get answers instantly. Existing staff stop re-asking the same questions. Consistent information across your whole team.

Faster onboarding. Fewer errors from inconsistent information. Less interruption of senior staff.

Document summarisation

Summarise patient records, referral letters, clinical notes, and reports quickly. Save time on documentation review and help practitioners get to the relevant information faster without reading entire documents from scratch.

Time saved in clinical administration. Faster preparation before appointments.

AI training for clinic teams

Practical workshops to help your clinical and admin team understand how to use AI tools safely and effectively. Covers AI basics, privacy in healthcare contexts, tools worth using, and how to identify AI opportunities in your specific workflow.

Team confident and capable with AI. Clear understanding of what is safe and appropriate in a healthcare context.

A note on privacy

Healthcare AI, done carefully.

Healthcare involves sensitive patient data. Every AI solution we build for clinics is designed with privacy, data handling, and appropriate access controls as a core consideration , not an afterthought.

We map what data is used, where it goes, and what safeguards are in place before anything is built. We build AI systems that support your team, not ones that create compliance risk.
