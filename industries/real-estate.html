Where AI helps

AI solutions for real estate.

Lead capture and qualification

An AI assistant that engages with buyer and seller enquiries the moment they land on your website — asking the right questions, capturing contact details, qualifying intent, and routing serious leads to you. Even while you are at an open home.

More leads captured. Better qualified before you call. Fewer missed opportunities outside business hours.

Property enquiry chatbots

An AI chatbot on your website or listing pages that answers property-specific questions — price guides, open home times, features, body corporate details, comparable sales — and books viewing appointments or appraisals directly into your calendar.

Faster enquiry responses. More bookings captured automatically. Buyers get answers immediately, not the next morning.

Open-home follow-up automation

Automated follow-up sequences for everyone who attended an open home — thanking them for coming, sharing relevant property information, asking for feedback, and identifying who is ready to move forward. Triggered automatically, no manual work required.

Every open-home attendee followed up consistently. Buyers who are ready surface faster. Fewer leads going cold after initial interest.

Listing content generation

AI-assisted drafting of property descriptions, email campaigns, social media posts, and print copy. You provide the property details and photos, the AI drafts compelling copy — you review and refine. Faster content production without sacrificing quality.

Listings on market faster. More consistent content quality. Less time writing from scratch for every property.

Appraisal request workflows

Automated workflows for capturing vendor appraisal requests, gathering property details in advance, sending confirmation and preparation emails, and following up after the appraisal is delivered. More professional, less manual.

More appraisals booked. Better-prepared meetings. Fewer dropped vendor relationships after the initial meeting.

Buyer and seller CRM automation

Keep buyers and sellers engaged throughout longer transaction timelines with automated nurture sequences — market updates, relevant new listings, milestone check-ins. Relationships maintained without manual tracking across dozens of active clients.

Stronger client relationships. Less time manually keeping in touch. More repeat and referral business over time.
