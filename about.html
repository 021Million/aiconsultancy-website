About

Helping businesses use AI in practical ways.

AI Consultancy was built to help organisations move past AI hype and into practical implementation. The goal is straightforward: help businesses understand where AI is useful, train teams to use it well, and build tailored solutions that genuinely improve how a business runs.

Why this business exists

Most businesses know AI matters. They have seen the headlines, tried ChatGPT, and heard that competitors are already using it. But turning that general awareness into something useful , specific tools, working automations, trained teams , is where it gets unclear.

The gap between "I know AI is a thing" and "I have AI working in my business" is where most businesses get stuck. Not because AI is hard, but because the path from curiosity to implementation is not obvious, and there are too many people selling hype instead of help.

Keira helps businesses close that gap. She works with business owners and teams to identify where AI creates real value in their specific context, train them to use AI well, and build practical tools that fit into how the business actually operates.

How we work

Every engagement starts with understanding the business. What does it do, how does it operate, where is the time going, and what problems are worth solving. From there we identify the highest-value AI opportunities , not every possible thing AI could do, but the things that will make a meaningful difference to how the business runs.

Implementation follows the same principle. Tools get built around existing workflows, not around what is technically interesting. The measure of success is not the sophistication of the AI , it is whether it saves time, reduces admin, and creates outcomes the business can see and measure.

We also care about doing this responsibly. Every system is built with privacy, human oversight, and clear documentation in mind , so teams can understand and manage what has been built, not just depend on it.

How we approach it

What guides every engagement.

Practical over impressive

The goal is AI that works in your business, not AI that looks good in a demo. We prioritise solutions that your team will actually use, that solve problems they actually have.

Privacy by design

Before anything is built, we map what data is involved, where it goes, who can access it, and what safeguards are needed. Privacy is not an afterthought.

Human oversight always

AI supports people , it does not replace judgement. Every system we build keeps people responsible for the decisions that matter.

Honest about what AI can do

AI is genuinely useful for certain things and genuinely not suited for others. We are direct about both , no overselling, no exaggeration.

Outcomes, not outputs

We care about time saved, admin reduced, response times improved, and revenue per employee increased , not the number of tools installed or automations deployed.

Documentation and ownership

Every system comes with plain-English documentation. Your team should understand what has been built, how it works, and how to manage it , not be dependent on us to keep the lights on.
